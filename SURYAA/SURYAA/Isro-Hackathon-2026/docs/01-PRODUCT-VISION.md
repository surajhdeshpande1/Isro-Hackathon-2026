# TAPAS SURYA DRISHTI - Product Vision

## Urban Climate Reasoning Intelligence System

### What is TAPAS SURYA DRISHTI?

TAPAS SURYA DRISHTI is not a dashboard, heatmap, GIS viewer, or smart-city portal.

**It is the world's first Urban Climate Reasoning Intelligence System.**

The system functions as a living climate intelligence platform capable of:
- Observing urban heat patterns
- Understanding root causes
- Researching historical evolution
- Validating conclusions
- Forecasting future scenarios
- Optimizing interventions
- Defending recommendations
- Continuously improving solutions

---

## Core Product Philosophy

**The map is the product.**
**The city is the interface.**
**Climate intelligence is the content.**
**Decision-making is the outcome.**

---

## Product Goals

Transform any city entered by a user into a living climate-aware digital twin capable of:

1. **Understanding** heat formation
2. **Identifying** heat hotspots
3. **Classifying** hotspot types
4. **Explaining** root causes
5. **Researching** historical evolution
6. **Finding** evidence
7. **Validating** conclusions
8. **Generating** optimal interventions
9. **Calculating** financial outcomes
10. **Creating** implementation roadmaps
11. **Forecasting** future scenarios
12. **Defending** recommendations
13. **Monitoring** future changes

---

## Non-Negotiable Product Rules

- Never display a hotspot without explaining why it exists
- Never generate a recommendation without evidence
- Never display a financial estimate without assumptions
- Never display a forecast without uncertainty ranges
- Never produce a recommendation without comparing alternatives
- Never hide confidence scores
- Never hide assumptions
- Never hide evidence
- Every recommendation must survive scientific scrutiny
- Every recommendation must survive government scrutiny
- Every recommendation must survive investor scrutiny
