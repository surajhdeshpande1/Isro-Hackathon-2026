## Hotspot Typologies

### Industrial Heat Island (IHI)
**Mechanism:** Anthropogenic heat sources
**Key Drivers:** Industrial activity, waste heat
**Optimal Interventions:** Waste heat recovery, cooling networks, industrial retrofits

### Street Canyon Heat Island (SCHI)
**Mechanism:** Geometry-trapped radiation
**Key Drivers:** Building density, geometry
**Optimal Interventions:** Radiative cooling, canyon shades, facades

### Dense Residential Heat Island (DRHI)
**Mechanism:** Diffuse impervious cover
**Key Drivers:** Building density, road surfaces
**Optimal Interventions:** Urban forestry, cool pavements, district cooling

### Transit Congestion Heat Island (TCHI)
**Mechanism:** Mobile heat + traffic
**Key Drivers:** Vehicle emissions, congestion
**Optimal Interventions:** Transit cooling, fog collection, mist towers

### Water Body Degradation Heat Island (LDHI)
**Mechanism:** Lost cooling function
**Key Drivers:** Water pollution, riparian loss
**Optimal Interventions:** Lake restoration, thermal storage, cold water irrigation

### Peripheral Sprawl Heat Island (PSHI)
**Mechanism:** Rapid urbanization
**Key Drivers:** Vegetation loss, new construction
**Optimal Interventions:** Green belts, vernacular architecture, wind corridors
